About Us

What started out as a humble business venture in 1974 has successfully evolved into a leading agro-commodity trading organisation. We have decades of trading experience in understanding the nuances of this business. We believe “Trust” to be the most important factor in building any business relations. We pride ourselves in building trust with 1000+ people across India in this trade. Another important aspect is “Consistent Quality”. We believe in consistently providing quality products to our customers. These 2 factors have also helped us in gaining trust of Modern Retail Chains in India for supply for quality products consistently since the past 20 years. We have partnered with manufacturing plants spread across India. Product quality is monitored right from processing of raw material to final delivery. Each step is cautiously checked for highest quality standards. Our products are hygienically packed to prevent contamination.
